\section*{Reproducibility statement}

We provide all the necessary information to reproduce our results.
In Section~\ref{sec:experiments}, we include details about our benchmarks and the implementation hyperparameters for \cresearcher{} and all the other baselines.
In Appendix~\ref{app:details}, we give details about our subset of the \kbenchsyz{} benchmark, and provide information about the crash reproduction setup, the unit test setup, compute resources, prompts, and the implementation of the SWE-agent and Agentless baselines.
In Appendix~\ref{app:ffmpeg}, we give the complete steps to recreate our dataset for \ffmpeg{} and reproduce our results.
We also present implementation details for \cresearcher{} in Appendix~\ref{app:design}.
Finally, we submit a supplementary zip file containing (a) the full code of our implementation, (b) prompts and config files used for \cresearcher{} and the SWE-agent and Agentless baselines, (c) both the datasets we used, (d) the patches produced by \cresearcher{} and each baseline, and (e) a \texttt{README} that explains how to reproduce our results.
